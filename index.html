Toyama Prefecture is a prefecture located in the Chubu region of Japan. The prefectural capital and the largest city is Toyama City.

Toyama seafood

Masuzushi - 鱒寿司 -

Masuzushi is a local dish of Toyama prefecture. A type of pressed sushi that is seasoned with vinegar without being fermented with trout. The best harmony that vinegared rice tightens the fat-rich trout. The supreme dish you definitely want to eat if you come to Toyama.

Shiroebi - 白えび -

Shiroebi is an endemic species that inhabits only the deep sea along the coast of Japan, and Toyama Bay on the Sea of Japan side is the only fishing ground. It is characterized by its moist body and the unique flavor of Shiroebi that cannot be expressed in words.

Hotaruika - ほたるいか -

Firefly squid is a mysterious creature surrounded by many mysteries whose whole body glows pale. I was born in spring and will end my life in a year. I usually live in deep water of 200-600m. Its taste goes well with alcohol and is often described as "devilish(悪魔的)".

Why is seafood delicious in Toyama?

Fish in Toyama Bay that have overcome the complex tides of the Sea of Japan and intense waves. It is moderately trained, firm and moderately greasy. Toyama Bay is layered with the warm Tsushima Current and cold deep sea water with a water temperature of 1 to 2 degrees Celsius throughout the year. There are abundant plankton that feed the fish, and delicious fish grow.
